How Email Scoringⓘ works

Email Scoringⓘ uses proprietary artificial intelligence to assess how likely a subscriber is to interact with your email campaign. This method is ideal for catch-all email addresses, which are impossible to validate outright and require further testing.

By uploading your catch-all lead or a list of catch-alls, each subscriber will receive an email quality score ranging from 1 to 10 (0 is unknown).

1- Low email engagement score; likely invalid or abandoned

10- High email engagement score; an active catch-all email address

Why Email Scoring tools are vital for catch-all validation

As you grow your email list, you’re likely to acquire catch-all email addresses.

Here’s the problem: no one can validate a catch-all email address outright. Emailing one could lead to another email bounce. As a result - most validators mark catch-alls as “risky” or “undeliverable” across the board.

But know this - Even one quality catch-all lead can lead to huge sales if the prospect is the right fit for your business.

That’s why we expanded our tools to provide you with an email score analysis to validate catch-all email addresses accurately.

You deserve the data to decide which leads you should keep and which you should abandon.
